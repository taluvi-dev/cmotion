The example on the [homepage](/). A 2D background composes with a 3D
scene built from one extruded glyph: three independent animations
drive its `y` rotation, hue, and scale; a low-amplitude `wave`
adds a slow `x`-axis sway. Lighting is one ambient plus two opposing
directionals.
