A close cousin of the [homepage](/) glyph, scaled up to a whole word.
`text.glyph("cmotion")` lays the string out glyph-by-glyph and
`extrude` turns the outlines into one 3D mesh; a 2D background
composes behind it. Three independent animations drive the hue, the
scale pulse, and a low-amplitude `wave` that nods it on `x`, while a
second `wave` gives the `y`-axis a gentle sway — a full spin would
leave the word mirrored for half the loop. Lighting is one ambient
plus two opposing directionals.
